struct Eigen\+::\+Tensor\+Sycl\+::internal\+::\+Functor\+Extractor$<$ Evaluator $>$}

struct \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_eigen_1_1_tensor_sycl_1_1internal_1_1_functor_extractor}{Functor\+Extractor}}\+: This struct is used to extract the functors constructed on the host-\/side, to pack them and reuse them in reconstruction of the expression on the device. We have to do that as in \mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_eigen}{Eigen}} the functors are not stateless so we cannot re-\/instantiate them on the device. We have to pass instantiated functors to the device.
